Controls - 'WASD', 'Q', and 'E'.

This is a little maze game where you must find your way to the center to see a special surprize.

I added support to add mazes to this game easily. One only needs to make 32x32 picture (and a little more code to make it selectedable).

The 32x32 png only needs to contain pixels with max transparency (a wall in the maze off random height between 1 and 4),

and any other pixel (dirt road). The creator only needs to remember the fixed location of the player and the 'surprise in the center'.

Right now, I have only added 2 maps to the pool. You can tell which map is selected by the image at the bottom of the screen.

If the mouse controls for the camera are too finiky, you can use WASD along with QE to move and control the camera respectfully.

Story: You hear noises coming from the heart of your maze garden. Noises from a creature you've never heard before. What could it be?

Could it be friendly... or dangerous? You know of only one way to find out.
